Phonon bands & bands-over-BZ surface

Right: 2D dispersion (the ruler). Left: 3D surface over the first Brillouin zone. Color = atom-displacement blend — each mode is tinted by which species move (by |e|² participation). Click a point on the right to drop a marker on the left and animate that mode in the atom panel below (displacements computed by the proven kinematics.js engine). The 3D surface is clickable too — this payload carries exact engine modes on an in-BZ grid (mesh_modes); clicking a band sheet snaps to the nearest node and animates that exact mode at the node's own q (shown in the readout — no eigenvector is ever interpolated). The node grid itself is hidden by default; the Nodes checkbox shows it.
